Deck: A collection of items that have their own space on the screen. The items that the player has
	out during encounters is a deck. The player's items they have when trading them out during the
	sorting state is a deck.

Hand: A deck that is out during an encounter such as the player's or a trader's

Pack: These are the items the player cannot access during encounters and must use them during the sort state

Inventory: Both the player's Hand and Pack combined. Whatever the player currently has on them
